Colours

#E98100

This colour is used as the primary colour of our brand. It is used to help draw attention as a bright colour as well as symbolising youth, vibrance and happiness.

#FFFFFF

This colour is used as our complementary colour to our primary colour. It is also used for our banner font colour as well as part of our logo.

#E8E8E8

This colour is used for part of our logo as well as our tertiary colour.

#333333

This colour is used as our footer and navigation bar colour, its also a complementary colour of our white font.

#3A597C

This colour is used for colour splash features e.g. for buttons, in order to draw attention to their importance. It is also a complementary colour of our primary brand colour.

Content

BuzzInMcr aims to achieve a friendly tour guide tone with use of sarcastic/ironic play on words within club/bar bios.

Our writing style will be short, concise and direct to the information that we believe customers will find helpful towards finding them a great nightout.

BuzzInMcr will try to achieve it's aim of creating a friendly, easy-to-understand web page by being a voice of knowledge of Manchester. This will be achieved by naming specific features of clubs and bars that tourists, new to the city, wouldn't know.
